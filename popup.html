Google Search Operators

Refine your Google searches using symbols or words to make your search results more precise

Search social media

Put @ in front of a word to search social media

@twitter apple @twitter hacking

Search for a price

Put $ in front of a number

camera $400 iphone $600

Search hashtags

Put # in front of a word

#throwbackthursday #foobar

Exclude words from your search

Put - in front of a word you want to leave out

jaguar speed -car

Search for an exact match

Put a word or phrase inside quotes

"tallest building" "Steve Jobs"

Search within a range of numbers

Put .. between two numbers

camera $50..$100 "hotel london" $100..$150

Combine searches

Put "OR" between each search query

marathon OR race car for sale ford OR mercedes

Search for a specific site

Put "site:" in front of a site or domain

site:youtube.com site:.gov site:bbc.co.uk mars

Search for related sites

Put "related:" in front of a web address you already know

related:time.com related:netflix.com

See Googles cached version of a site

Put "cache:" in front of the site address

cache:example.com
